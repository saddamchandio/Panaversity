# Reference — Letters

Templates and worked examples for the everyday outward genres. All share the skeleton in SKILL.md (header → addressee → Subject → Ref → body → closing → signature → CC → footer). Only the body shape and tone differ. Placeholders in `[brackets]` must be supplied or flagged.

---

## 1. Request letter (the workhorse)

**Shape:** open by situating the ask in context → state the problem/status → make the request with `may please be` → close on cooperation.

**Worked example (energization request):**

> **Chief Executive Officer,**
> **HESCO Hyderabad.**
>
> **Subject:**  **Request for Completion and Energization of Newly Constructed 11KV Feeder to RCC(S) ISMO Jamshoro:**
> **New Connection of 750KW under Tariff C-2 in favor of Chief Engineer (Southern Network & VRE Operation) RCC (South) NTDC Jamshoro as a Back Up Supply Through New Independent/Dedicated Feeder having a length of 1.8km to be emanated from 132kV Grid Station Jamshoro (On Cost Deposit Basis).**
>
> **Ref:**  i. Chief Engineer (P&E) HESCO Hyderabad office, letter no. [____]  Dated: [__.__.____]
>      ii. This office letter no. [____]  Dated: [__.__.____]
>
> Reference is made to the ongoing work of establishing a dedicated 11KV independent feeder from 132KV HESCO Grid Station Jamshoro to RCC(S) ISMO Jamshoro. It is highly appreciated that the majority of civil and structural work including erection of poles and stringing has been completed through the efforts of HESCO field teams. However, it is apprised that the installation of span and conductor on the terminal pole at the RCC(S) end remains pending, due to which final interconnection is yet to be established.
>
> Moreover, … therefore, in view of the on-going work and present situation, it is humbly requested that:
> - The remaining work … may kindly be expedited.
> - The already completed portion of the 11KV line may be energized provisionally, until the remaining works are fully accomplished.
>
> This initiative would not only support ISMO in ensuring power readiness and operational continuity, but would also result in preventing any theft of the 11KV equipment.
>
> **Your kind cooperation in this regard shall be highly appreciated.**
>
> [signature block] [CC]

**Notes:** lead with appreciation of work done before naming what's pending; bullet the asks when there is more than one; add the standing project descriptor as the bold sub-subject whenever the matter belongs to a named project.

## 2. Reminder (REMINDER – I / II)

**Shape:** identical skeleton + urgency marker; recall the chain; name the lapse without rudeness; restate "once again."

**Distinctive lines:**
- Marker right-aligned on the addressee's first line: `REMINDER – I` (or `– II`), or `Matter Most Urgent`.
- `In the continuation of above referred letter at Sr.No.(iii), a representative of our office met your good self … but regretfully no progress has been observed so far.`
- `It is therefore once again requested that the relevant staff may please be advised for completion of the remaining work …`
- Subject often prefixed `Reminder:` and a fuller reference chain (i–vi) is carried.

## 3. Forwarding / submission note

**Shape:** very short. Often carries an `Attention:` line to the dealing officer. Forward the enclosed case and ask for action.

**Worked example:**

> **Executive Director (System Operations),**
> **ISMO, Islamabad.**
>
> **Attention:    Dy. Director (HR & A) ISMO, Islamabad**
>
> **Subject:**  **[Request for Fees Reimbursement … in respect of Mr. Saddam Hussain Dy. Director (Tech) ISMO Jamshoro.]**
>
> **Ref:**  The application of [Mr. ____], dt: [__.__.____]
>
> Enclosed please find herewith the self-explanatory application of **Mr. [____], [designation]** of this office regarding [subject matter]. The application is hereby forwarded for necessary action as per rules, please.
>
> **D/A: as above.**
>
> [signature] [CC ending: Officer concerned. / Master File.]

**Variants:** `Enclosed please find herewith an undertaking on non-judicial stamp paper as per prescribed format …`; `Enclosed please find herewith the complete case of Mr. [____] … w.e.f. [date] after incorporating admissible pay and allowances as per rules.` Enclosure list uses `a. b. c. d.` (HRIS profile, exam result, certificate copies, salary slip, attested degree, NOC, etc.).

## 4. Billing / administrative letter (NTN update, bill correction)

**Shape:** often a tidy dash-list body. State the user's position, give the corrected particulars in bold, request the update, close.

**Distinctive lines:**
- `In the light of above referred letter (copy attached) it is apprised that:`
- `Please be advised that our office is now functionally separate from NTDC and operates as an independent entity i.e: **Independent System & Market Operator of Pakistan (Guarantee) Limited (ISMO)**. The Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) has issued ISMO its own National Tax Number (NTN): **[H239814]**.`
- For corrections: `… which clearly establishes a typographical error on part of [HESCO] … it is requested that the concerned quarters may kindly be advised to cause the rectification of the said error and arrange for the issuance of a revised corrected bill at the earliest.`
- Liability hedge: `any delay in addressing this matter may result in unintended financial liabilities, including late payment surcharges or audit observations, for which this office shall not be held responsible.`
- Close: `Thank you for your cooperation.` or the standard cooperation line.

## 5. Appreciation letter

**Register shifts up.** First-person `I`/`We` is allowed and expected. No Ref block. Elevated, generous diction. No CC action tags needed beyond the usual courtesy copies.

**Worked example (tone reference):**

> **[Engr. ____],**
> **[Designation],**
> **ISMO – RCC(S) Jamshoro.**
>
> **Subject:**  **Letter of Appreciation – [Outstanding Contribution to … ]**
>
> It is with profound appreciation that I acknowledge your exceptional initiative-taking and technical prowess in the conceptualization, development, and flawless deployment of [the achievement]. This initiative is not merely a project completion; it represents a pivotal stride in our strategic digitalization roadmap.
>
> Your unwavering commitment to quickly adopt higher management's vision, harnessing technology as a transformative force, coupled with your remarkable ability to navigate complex technical challenges, has set a new benchmark for innovation within our organization. …
>
> Your extraordinary contribution is highly appreciated. We eagerly anticipate your continued contributions to our collective success.
>
> [signature] [CC]

## 6. To Whom It May Concern (certificate)

**No addressee, no Ref.** Centered heading. Single declarative paragraph. Officer named in bold with full designation. Purpose stated plainly.

**Worked example:**

> **TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN**
>
> The undersigned is pleased to inform that **Engr. [____] [Designation]** of this office has been granted permission to [state the fact / permission], [purpose, e.g. "only for educational purposes for his Master Research Thesis: **"[THESIS TITLE]"**, as partial completion requisite of his ME (Energy Systems Engineering) degree program from MUET, Jamshoro"].
>
> [signature] 
> **CC**
> - Officer concerned.
> - Master File.
