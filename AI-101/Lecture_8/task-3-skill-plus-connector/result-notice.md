**NOTICE**

**Subject:  Introduction of Community Transport Facility under the Newly Approved ISMO Community Transport Policy**

This is to inform all employees of ISMO – RCC(S) Jamshoro that the Board of Directors, vide **Board Resolution IV/17 dated 15th June 2026**, has approved the **ISMO Community Transport Policy** covering employee and school/college transport. The salient features are apprised as under:

**Eligibility**
- Applicable to all regular ISMO employees on an optional, first-come first-served basis, on payment, subject to availability of seats/vehicles on approved routes.
- School/college transport is admissible only for children of employees residing in official ISMO colonies, aged **07 years & above** and enrolled in a recognized institution, up to a maximum of **three (03) children** per employee, duly approved by the Chief (HR & Admin) Officer.

**Monthly Fee Structure (PKR)**

| Grade Category | Employee (Pick & Drop) | Per Child (Pick & Drop) |
|---|---|---|
| G-1 to G-3 | 2,000 | 1,500 |
| G-4 to G-6 | 2,500 | 2,000 |
| G-7 & Above | 4,000 | 3,000 |

Employees desirous of availing the facility may please submit their applications to the **Chief (HR & Admin) Officer** through the proper official channel. Allocation shall be made by the **ISMO Transport Committee** strictly on approved routes, subject to availability of seats. A driver log book shall be maintained for each trip.

**We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.**
